Built for Competition

Most runners waste their potential on solo runs with zero accountability. Katzu transforms every run into a battle—where your opponents are waiting, your team is counting on you, and every day matters.

Team Battles

Form elite squads of 3 or 5. Face matched opponents daily. Dual competition—team outcome plus personal 1v1 matchup—means you're never running alone. Your squad expects you to show up.

Transparent Scoring

KTX score based on heart rate zones + elevation. Zero black boxes. Know exactly how your effort translates to points. Fair competition demands transparent rules—no algorithms hiding the truth.

Daily Streaks

Build unbroken streaks that become your identity. Loss aversion keeps you consistent—breaking a 30-day streak hurts worse than any single missed workout. That's the point.

MVP Recognition

4 ways to win every battle: Highest KTX, Longest Distance, Best Pace, Most Improved. Multiple paths to victory mean every runner gets their moment. Dominance comes in many forms.

Public Profiles

Every runner gets katzu.org/@username. Build your competitive reputation. Share victories, flex lifetime stats, let the world see what you're made of. Your numbers tell your story.

Works with Any Tracker

Apple Watch, Garmin, Fitbit, Polar, Wahoo, Suunto—even phone GPS. Use what you already own. Zero hardware lock-in, zero subscriptions. Just pure competition with the gear you trust.
